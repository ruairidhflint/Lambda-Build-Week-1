How it Works

Pintereach is simple and intuitive to use, but just in case, follow along below with the basic steps and you'll be organizing your research in no time.

Pintereach

Not a member? Sign up!

Step 1:
Create an Account

Use the link at the bottom of the page to sign up and create an account. It's free and takes less than a minute. If you already have one, log in!

Step 2:
Make your First Board

Once you're in, click the "create a board" icon. Give it a name and a few keywords and you're ready to add content.

Step 3:
Add your Content

Post links, videos, any kind of content to your boards using the "add content" button. The app will render it for clear viewing.

Step 4:
Access Anywhere

Profit! You're good to go anywhere, anytime. Your content is just a click away with our apps or any browser you can find.
